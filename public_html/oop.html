Inheritance

Along with Bob Kahn, Vinton G. Cerf is considered one of the “fathers of the Internet”. The two partners came up with the TCP/IP protocol in 1974, working on Leonard Kleinrock's networking group connecting the first two nodes of ARPAnet. Cerf also helped form the Internet Society and ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers).
